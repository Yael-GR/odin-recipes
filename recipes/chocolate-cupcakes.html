Chocolate Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups raising flour
  • 4.5 tbsp sugar
  • 100g melted butter
  • 4 eggs (2 eggs and 2 egg yolks)
  • 150g chocolate milk

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 175C.
  2. Melt the chocolate milk in a small bowl.
  3. In a large bowl, mix all ingredients.
  4. Pour batter into a cupcake pan.
  5. Bake for 9 minutes.
