S'mores Cookies

Link to the original recipe

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1/2 ts baking powder
  • 1/2 ts baking soda
  • 1 ts cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips
  • 140g softened butter
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ts vanilla extract
  • 1 pack of marshmallows
  • 1 bar chocolate milk
  • 4 graham crackers

Steps

  1. Roughly chop the chocolate bar and graham crackers, and tear the marshmallows. Put them to the side in bowls.
  2. Combine the flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda and cornstarch in a bowl.
  3. In a large bowl, beat together the butter and sugars for about 2 minutes.
  4. Add the egg and vanilla and beat until combined.
  5. Slowly add the dry mixture in, beating on low until combined.
  6. Set a bit of marshmallows and chocolate to the side, and mix the rest and the chocolate chips and graham crackers into the dough.
  7. Chill in the fridge for 1 hour.
  8. Heat oven to 180C.
  9. Shape the cookies into flattened balls, and bake for 7 minutes, add some marshmallows and chocolate then continue baking for 2 minutes.
