Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 200g melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup chocolate milk mix
  • 1 yogurt
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 ts baking powder

  • 1 pack heavy cream
  • 1/2 pack dark chocolate

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 170C.
  2. In a bowl mix the butter, sugar, eggs, chocolate milk mix and yogurt.
  3. Add the flour and baking powder, then beat until a smooth batter.
  4. Oil a baking pan and pour the batter in.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Mix the heavy cream and dark chocolate to make the coating, and pour it over the cake.
