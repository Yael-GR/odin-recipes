Mac n Cheese

Ingredients

  • 225g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 2 cups cheder cheese

  • 1/4 ts dry thyme
  • 1/4 ts ground mustard
  • 1/4 ts salt
  • 1/4 ts black pepper

  • Parmesan cheese
  • Chilli Flakes

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pot on medium hit until bubbling.
  2. Brown the onion in the pot for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic for 1 minute.
  4. Add the milk, chicken broth and water and wait for boil.
  5. Add the pasta and move to low heat.
  6. 5 minutes before the pasta is ready, add the spices.
  7. After pasta is done cooking, remove from heat and mix in the cheese.
  8. Plate the pasta, and add the parmesan cheese and chilli flakes if you like.
