Snickerdoodle Cookies

Link to the original recipe

Ingredients

  • 2.75 cups flour
  • 2 ts baking powder
  • 1/2 ts vinegar
  • 1/2 ts salt
  • 225g softened butter
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ts vanilla extract

  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 180C.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder, vinegar, and salt.
  3. In another large bowl, cream together butter and sugar. Add the eggs and vanilla and mix again.
  4. Add in the dry ingredients and mix.
  5. In a small bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  6. Shape the dough into small balls.
  7. roll each ball in the sugar and cinnamon mixture.
  8. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
