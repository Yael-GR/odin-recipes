Butter Cookies

Link to the original recipe

Ingredients

  • 175g soft butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 pack vanilla sugar
  • 2 eggs (1 egg and 1 egg yolk)
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 ts baking powder
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix the butter, sugar and vanilla sugar into a uniformed mixture.
  2. Add the egg and egg yolk, then mix.
  3. Add the flour, baking powder and salt, then knead into a dough.
  4. Flatten the dough into a thick disc, wrap in saran wrap and chill for 30 minutes.
  5. After chilling, heat the oven to 175C.
  6. Knead the dough for a bit, roll it and cut out shapes.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes, cookies should stay white and not shiny..
