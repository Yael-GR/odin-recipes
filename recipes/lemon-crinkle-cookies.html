Lemon Crinkle Cookies

Link to the original recipe

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 70g warm melted butter
  • 1 ts vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 240g flour
  • 1 ts baking powder
  • 1/4 ts salt

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup icing sugar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, add the lemon zest and sugar and press together with fingers.
  2. Add the melted butter and vanilla, then whisk until combined.
  3. Add the eggs and whisk.
  4. Add the lemon juice and whisk until smooth.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder and salt. Add them to the wet ingredients.
  6. Mix until a dough is created. At this point the dough should be sticky and almost runny.
  7. Chill dough in the fridge for at least 2 hours..
  8. Heat oven to 180C.
  9. Shape the dough into small balls.
  10. Roll each bowl in sugar, then in the icing sugar.
  11. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
