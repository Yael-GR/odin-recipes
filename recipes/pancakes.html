Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raising flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix all of the ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Butter or oil a pan.
  3. Fry the pancakes on low heat.
  4. Flip them when the edges brown and the middle isn't too runny.
