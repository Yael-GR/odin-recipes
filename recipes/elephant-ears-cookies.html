Elephant Ears Cookies

Ingredients

  • Puff pastry
  • Granulated sugar
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Spread sugar over the counter top.
  2. Carefully place the puff pastry over the sugar, and roll it a bit.
  3. Spread sugar and cinnamon over the dough.
  4. Cut the long side in half, and roll each half from both sides to create the shape.
  5. Place in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  6. Heat oven to 160C.
  7. Cut the rolls to thin slices, around half a centimeter.
  8. BAke for 15 minutes, until slightly browned.
