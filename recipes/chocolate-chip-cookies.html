CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

Description

This detailed recipe will instruct you on how to bake the most delicious chocolate chip cookies. Your house will be filled with the amazing smells of addictive dough, and your family will beg you to make these cookies again as soon as there are no more left (and believe me, that won't take long at all).

Ingredients

  • 3 cups self raising flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 ts salt
  • 1 ts baking soda
  • 200g melted butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 ts vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 175 degrees Celsius.
  2. In a large bowl, mix all ingredients except for the chocolate chips until combined.
  3. Add the chocolate chips to the bowl and mix again.
  4. Roll the dough in to small balls and place them on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake the cookies in the oven for 11 minutes.
  6. Let the bakes cookies cool for a few minutes, before storing them in an ait-tight container.
